# Note

GCD stands for Greatest Common Divisor or Highest Common Factor:
> It is the largest positive integer that divides both numbers fully, ie. without any remainder. 

We use Euclidean method.

![](/assets/images/gcd.png)

Sample input: 54,24
output: 6
